The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Devil Wears Prada [2006]

With an aspiration to become a journalist, Andy, a young graduate, travels to New York. She starts working as an assistant to one of the city's biggest magazine editors, the cynical Miranda Priestly.

The Wolf of Wall Street [2013]

Introduced to life in the fast lane through stockbroking, Jordan Belfort takes a hit after a Wall Street crash. He teams up with Donnie Azoff, cheating his way to the top as his relationships slide.

La La Land [2016]

La La Land is a lovely, pleasing, and showstopping movie that should be watched on the biggest screen,loudest sound, and brightest colors as possible. A celebration of music and magic, where swelling emotions are captured by somber yet jazzy. Features a career best performance from Emma Stone.
